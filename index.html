पंचकर्म · Panchakarma

Ancient Healing
Rediscovered

Panchakarma is a time-tested Ayurvedic purification system that rejuvenates body, mind and spirit at the cellular level. The word "Panchakarma" means five actions (Pancha = five, Karma = actions) — five therapeutic procedures that systematically eliminate accumulated toxins (ama) from the body.

Dr. Saloni blends classical Panchakarma with modern diagnostics for deeply personalised healing. Every consultation begins with Nadi Parikshan — the ancient art of pulse diagnosis — to understand your unique Prakriti (constitution) and Vikriti (current imbalance).
